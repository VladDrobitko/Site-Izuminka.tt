Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how izuminka.tt ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website or book tattoo services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

When you book a consultation or tattoo session, we collect:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number)
  • Tattoo design preferences and requirements
  • Preferred appointment dates and times
  • Medical information relevant to tattooing (allergies, medications)
  • Age verification (you must be 18+)
  • Previous tattoo experience
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may collect:

  • IP address and general location information
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Referral source (how you found our website)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Schedule and manage tattoo appointments
  • Communicate about your tattoo design and session
  • Provide aftercare instructions and support
  • Send appointment reminders and confirmations
  • Improve our services and website experience
  • Display completed work in portfolio (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under GDPR, we process your personal data based on:

  • Consent: When you book services or consent to portfolio use
  • Contract performance: To fulfill tattoo service agreements
  • Legitimate interests: To improve services and communicate effectively
  • Legal compliance: To meet health and safety regulations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information only:

  • With trusted service providers (appointment booking, email services)
  • When required by law or health authorities
  • To protect our legal rights or safety
  • With your explicit consent for portfolio display

5. Portfolio and Social Media Use

With your explicit consent, we may:

  • Photograph your completed tattoo for portfolio purposes
  • Share images on our website and social media
  • Use images for promotional materials
  • Note: Your face and identifying features will not be shown without separate consent

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure data storage and transmission
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Proper disposal of physical records

7. Data Retention

We retain your information:

  • Client records: 7 years (health and safety requirements)
  • Appointment data: 2 years after last session
  • Portfolio images: Until you request removal
  • Marketing communications: Until you unsubscribe

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Data portability: Transfer your data to another provider
  • Object: Object to processing for marketing purposes
  • Withdraw consent: Remove consent for portfolio use

9. Cookies and Website Analytics

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Essential website functionality
  • Remembering your preferences
  • Basic analytics (with your consent)

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

10. Third-Party Services

Our website may use:

  • Netlify (hosting and forms)
  • Google Fonts (typography)
  • Instagram integration
  • Analytics services (with consent)

11. Age Requirements

Our services are only for individuals 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

12. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries outside the EU. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified via email or website notice.

14. Contact Information

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your rights:

For GDPR requests, please include "Privacy Request" in the subject line and specify your request type.

This Privacy Policy complies with GDPR and other applicable data protection laws. You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you have concerns about how your data is handled.
