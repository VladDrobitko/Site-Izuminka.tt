4. Health and Safety Requirements

Mandatory Health Disclosure

You MUST inform us of any:

  • Allergies (especially to latex, metals, or inks)
  • Skin conditions (eczema, psoriasis, dermatitis)
  • Blood disorders or clotting issues
  • Current medications (especially blood thinners)
  • Autoimmune conditions or immunosuppression
  • Heart conditions or pacemakers
  • Diabetes or other chronic conditions
  • Pregnancy or breastfeeding
  • Recent illnesses or infections

Age and Identification

  • Minimum age: 18 years (strictly enforced)
  • Valid government-issued photo ID required
  • No exceptions for parental consent
  • Fake ID will result in immediate service termination

Pre-Session Preparation

  • Eat a substantial meal 1-2 hours before appointment
  • Stay well-hydrated (water, not alcohol)
  • Get adequate sleep the night before
  • Avoid alcohol for 24 hours prior to session
  • Do not take blood thinners (aspirin, ibuprofen) 24 hours prior
  • Moisturize tattoo area for 3 days before session
  • Wear comfortable clothing that allows access to tattoo area
