Client Health Responsibilities

You must inform us of:

  • Any allergies or skin sensitivities

  • Recent illness or infections

  • Previous adverse reactions to tattoos

Pre-Session Requirements

  • Eat a substantial meal before your appointment
  • Stay hydrated and get adequate sleep
  • Avoid alcohol and blood thinners 24 hours prior
  • Clean and moisturize the tattoo area
  • Wear appropriate clothing for tattoo placement
